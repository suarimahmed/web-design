Welcome back Detective Jake Peralta


40 year old Jake is a detective at the 99th Precinct in Brooklyn, New York City. He works here full time. His job requires him to be at new crime scenes and to assess it in order to find the criminal that committed the crime.


He is considered the best detective in his precinct as he has more arrests than any other detective in the precinct. He currently receives cases when the captain or his sergeant assigns one to him, this is all done through paper files and most of the work does not require a computer. He is interested in using a web service that keeps a track of all his cases and can also automatically assign a case to him when a new one comes up. He heard the web service is good in keeping all cases (active, not-yet assigned and closed) organised for him.


The reason this interests him is because he is not one to be organised and is famously known for being a messy and unorganised person, it is something he is made fun of throughout his time as a detective, especially how unorganised his desk and his stack of cases is.
